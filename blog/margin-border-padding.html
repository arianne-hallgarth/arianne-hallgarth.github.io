Observe this box

There are Four Layers in the box shown.

Let's start from the middle, The "Content", It is where the text and images appear, in this case the text states "Observe this box" Next layer is called "Padding" it's the space between the margin and the content, it is used to create space between the margin and the content. This is transparent but one good way to test this is to adjust it's value in your stylesheet. Next we got the "Border", same as how you put border in your normal document, you specify its width, style and color in this sample, it is 50px-thick solid-style orange-color. And lastly, we have a "Margin", another familiar style that we use on our normal documents, it is also transparent, but technically, it clears out some space outside the border.
