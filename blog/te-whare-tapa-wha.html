"Te Whare Tapa Whā"

Here is my wellbeing plan base on Tā Mason Durie’s Te Whare Tapa Whā model.


Taha Tinana- Physical Wellbeing

For my personal well being: My plan is to go to the gym atleast 3x a week.
Go back on eating low carbs. And try to go back in a healthy sleep cycle to also be ready for bootcamp.

Taha Whānau- Family Wellbeing

For my family well being, I will find time to connect to people that make me feel like home, my husband, siblings, relatives and friends.

Taha Hinengaro- Emotional and Mental Wellbeing

For my emotional and mental well being: I will practice and remind myself of the growth-mindset.
I will keep a journal to be able to express myself freely without the need for affirmation from anyone.

Taha Wairua- Spiritual Wellbeing

For my spiritual well-being. I would find time to be able to go to church and discipleships and read the bible even just for a short period each day.

Whenua- Interconnections to the land and environment

For my whenua well being, I would continue to connect with nature and try to be more connected by taking time to listen, smell and feel nature apart from my normal visual appreciation.
